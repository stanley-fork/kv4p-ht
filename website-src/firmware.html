Firmware Flasher

Update your kv4p HT hardware directly from your browser

Before you begin:

  • Use Chrome, Chromium, or Edge on your computer (mobile browsers won't work)
  • Connect your kv4p HT to your computer via USB-C cable
  • When prompted, select "CP2102 USB to UART Bridge Controller"
  • Device not appearing? You may need the CP210x driver

What are you flashing?

Most people only need to flash Firmware. If you built your kv4p HT from scratch, then flash NVS as well.

Note: If you purchased a kit, NVS is likely already configured. Only flash this if you're building from scratch, or the kit maker didn't flash it.
